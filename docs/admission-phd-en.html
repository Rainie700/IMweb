PhD Program Admission Information

The PhD program emphasizes in-depth research and innovation, providing specialized academic resources for students aspiring to become academic researchers or professional technical experts.

Eligibility

  • Graduates with a master's degree or equivalent academic qualifications.
  • Applicants with research experience in related fields of information management will be given priority.

Application Documents

  • Master's transcripts
  • Three recommendation letters
  • Research proposal
  • Academic publications or research achievements

Research Areas and Resources

The PhD program covers diverse research areas, including smart cities, enterprise information systems, artificial intelligence applications, and information security. The department provides abundant research resources and encourages students to participate in international conferences and publish papers.
