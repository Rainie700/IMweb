Course Content

The Department of Information Management offers a comprehensive curriculum covering areas such as data science, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence, aiming to equip students with problem-solving and analytical skills.

Basic Courses

Below are some fundamental courses designed to build a solid foundation in information management:

Introduction to Information Management

Course Description: An introduction to the basic concepts of information management, including the design and management of information systems and the application of information technology.

Credits: 3 credits

Fundamentals of Programming

Course Description: Learn the basic concepts of programming, including syntax, data structures, and fundamental algorithms.

Credits: 4 credits

Data Structures

Course Description: Learn how to effectively organize and manage data, understanding the features and applications of various data structures.

Credits: 3 credits

Advanced Courses

Advanced courses allow students to deepen their knowledge in specialized fields and enhance their practical skills:

Artificial Intelligence

Course Description: An introduction to the basics of artificial intelligence, including machine learning and deep learning technologies.

Credits: 3 credits

Database Management Systems

Course Description: Learn the design and management of databases and how to manipulate databases using SQL.

Credits: 3 credits

Information Security Management

Course Description: Study the fundamental concepts of information security, including network security and encryption technologies.

Credits: 3 credits

Professional Courses

Professional courses focus on developing in-depth knowledge in the field of information management:

E-Commerce

Course Description: Learn how to design and manage online business platforms, including website development and payment systems.

Credits: 3 credits

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

Course Description: Introduction to the concepts and practical applications of enterprise resource planning, learning how to integrate enterprise resources.

Credits: 3 credits

Cloud Computing and Big Data

Course Description: Learn cloud computing technologies and explore scenarios and technologies for big data applications.

Credits: 3 credits
