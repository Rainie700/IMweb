Master's Program Affairs

Master's students are not only focused on academic research but also receive various support to excel in both academia and practice.

Academic Resources

  • Professional Databases: Master's students can access the school's professional databases for in-depth literature searches and research.
  • Research Laboratories: The department is equipped with advanced research facilities, allowing master's students to participate in various research projects and enhance their research skills.
  • Academic Writing Guidance: The university offers professional academic writing guidance to help students improve their thesis quality and prepare for academic publications.

Graduate Community

  • Academic Conferences: Regular academic seminars are held, encouraging students to share research results and learn from each other.
  • Interdisciplinary Collaboration: The department supports interdisciplinary research and discussions between master's students and students from other fields, fostering innovative thinking.
  • Peer Support and Feedback: The graduate community provides a platform for students to exchange ideas and support each other, promoting academic growth.

External Collaboration Opportunities

  • Industry-Academia Cooperation Programs: Master's students can participate in external industry-academia cooperation programs to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems and engage in professional practice.
  • Internship Opportunities: Through collaborations with both internal and external organizations, master's students are provided with more internship and workplace experience, facilitating future career development.
