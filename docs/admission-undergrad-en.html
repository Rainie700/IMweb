Undergraduate Admission Information

The Department of Information Management is committed to cultivating outstanding students with professional knowledge in information technology. We offer a wide range of courses covering fields such as data science, programming, and information security.

Eligibility

  • Graduating high school students or individuals with equivalent academic qualifications.
  • Applicants who pass the academic aptitude test and meet the department's required scores.

Application Documents

  • High school transcripts
  • Two recommendation letters
  • Academic aptitude test results
  • Personal resume and autobiography

Course Introduction

Our department offers comprehensive foundational and professional courses to help students establish a solid foundation in the field of information management. Courses include data structures, software development, and enterprise resource planning.
