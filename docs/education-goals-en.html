Department Mission

Our educational objective is to cultivate professional talents with excellent information management skills, innovative thinking, and a global perspective. Students will learn how to apply information technology to solve real-world problems and possess comprehensive abilities in business management and technology application, enabling them to stand out in the rapidly changing digital world.

Professional Knowledge and Technical Practice

The department's curriculum emphasizes the integration of practical operation and theoretical learning. Students will not only gain foundational knowledge in information technology but also delve into specialized fields such as database management, artificial intelligence, big data processing, and enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems. We expect students to master the techniques through practice and use this knowledge to address various challenges in business management.

Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration and Innovative Thinking

In this dynamic and competitive environment, innovation has become the key to business success. The department is committed to fostering students' innovative abilities by promoting interdisciplinary learning and collaboration, allowing students to engage in creative thinking across diverse professional fields and propose innovative solutions, while also being able to effectively implement and drive innovation.

Global Perspective and International Competitiveness

We believe that future information management professionals must possess a global perspective to handle diverse and cross-cultural challenges. Therefore, the department actively promotes international education, offering students opportunities for international exchange, student exchange programs, and overseas internships. Students will not only learn cutting-edge technology and management knowledge domestically but also broaden their horizons and enhance their competitiveness in the global workforce.

Fostering Corporate Responsibility and Ethical Values

As future information management professionals, we emphasize that students should possess high ethical standards and maintain a strong sense of responsibility in the information management process. Students will learn how to address issues such as data privacy and information security, adhering to the basic principles of corporate social responsibility, contributing to the sustainable development of enterprises.

Academic and Career Development in Parallel

Our educational objectives not only aim to cultivate students' academic abilities but also focus on their career development. The department offers various career planning services to help students connect their academic knowledge with the workplace, ensuring they can successfully apply what they have learned in real-world work situations and continue to grow in their future careers.
