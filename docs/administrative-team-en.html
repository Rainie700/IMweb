Our Team

The department's administrative team consists of a group of dedicated staff with professional backgrounds who are committed to providing academic and administrative support, ensuring that students can thrive in an excellent learning environment.

Leadership

The department chair and vice chair are responsible for the overall operation and development of the department. They have extensive experience in both academia and industry and lead the department toward excellence.

  • Department Chair: Professor Lin - Expert in information systems and management with many years of experience in industry-academia collaboration, committed to building a bridge between academia and industry.
  • Vice Chair: Dr. Chen - Focuses on data science and artificial intelligence, actively promoting the department's research development and innovation.

Administrative Support Team

To ensure the smooth operation of the department, we have a professional administrative support team, including academic assistants, research assistants, and the department office. These team members work together to support the department's daily operations and student services.

  • Academic Assistant: Responsible for student course arrangements and academic record management, providing daily academic support to students.
  • Administrative Assistant: Assists in planning and executing various department activities and handling related administrative matters.
