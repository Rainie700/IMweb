Master's Program Admission Information

The Master's program focuses on advanced knowledge and research skills in information management, offering abundant research resources and practice-oriented courses in cutting-edge fields such as artificial intelligence, big data, and cybersecurity.

Eligibility

  • Graduates with a bachelor's degree or equivalent academic qualifications.
  • Background in information management, computer science, or related disciplines.

Application Documents

  • Undergraduate transcripts
  • Three recommendation letters
  • Personal research proposal
  • Proof of academic achievements or relevant experience

Research Areas

The Master's program research areas cover data mining, data analytics, blockchain applications, e-commerce, and more. Students will have the opportunity to participate in diverse research projects and collaborate with industry partners.
